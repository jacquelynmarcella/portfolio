Dreamstate

React.js, Mogoose/MongoDB, Node.js, Express.js, Material UI

Live Site Github

Team Members: Antonia Villa and Ashton Christie

Primary Role: Front End Developer

Have you ever wondered what your dreams mean, or how your dreams change over time? This MERN stack app provides data visualizations to help users better understand their dreams. To conduct analysis, our team used the AWS Sentiment Analysis API and data scraping. By working with the Recharts library, we created data visualizations of individual dreams, sentiment over time, and commonly occuring symbols in the user's dreams. My primary contributions to this project were concepting the overall design direction, front end and responsive development, data scraping, and implenting the CRUD routes through Mongoose and React. Our group practiced pair programming in the initial sprint and managed workloads through Trello on subsequent sprints.
