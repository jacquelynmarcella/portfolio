Plant Parenthood

Node.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL, Sequelize, jQuery, Semantic UI

Go to Site View on Github

This Node/Express app uses web scraping to compile a PostgreSQL database of over 200 indoor houseplants and their care needs. RESTful routing allows users to easily view care needs of plants they've saved, journal about their plants, comment on plants for input from other users, and edit entries as needed. The target user for this app is the growing number of indoor houseplant enthusiasts. These are not master gardeners, they are people who enjoy adding greenery to their space but don't want to spend tons of time reading lengthy articles.
